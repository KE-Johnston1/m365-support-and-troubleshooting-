Teams Common Fixes
Scenario
Teams microphone not working

What the user reports
“No one can hear me in meetings.”

Steps taken
Check device settings in Teams
Select the correct microphone
Run a test call
Restart Teams
Audio works normally

Scenario
Teams stuck on connecting

What the user reports
“Teams won’t load. It just says connecting.”

Steps taken
Close Teams completely
Clear Teams cache
Restart device
Teams loads successfully

Scenario
Teams meetings not appearing in the calendar

What the user reports
“I can’t see any of my meetings in Teams.”

Steps taken
Confirm Outlook and Teams use the same account
Check Teams calendar permissions
Clear Teams cache
Restart Teams
Meetings appear correctly

Scenario
Teams desktop app works but web version does not

What the user reports
“I can join meetings in the app but not in the browser.”

Steps taken
Clear browser cache
Disable extensions
Check third party cookies
Try private browsing
User joins meeting successfully

Scenario
Teams presence stuck on away

What the user reports
“My status stays on away even when I’m active.”

Steps taken
Check Teams settings
Confirm Outlook is running
Clear Teams cache
Restart device
Presence updates correctly
